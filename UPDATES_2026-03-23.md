## Questions & Answers

**Q: Can I still have automatic audio?**
A: The automatic TTS has been removed, but you can easily add pre-recorded audio by playing an MP3 file alongside the demo.

**Q: How do I sync my recording with the demo?**
A: The script includes exact timing cues. Start your audio 2 seconds after page load, click "Play Demo" at 22 seconds, and it will sync perfectly at Normal (1x) speed.

**Q: What if I want a longer or shorter demo?**
A: Adjust the playback speed:
- Slower demo = more time for narration details
- Faster demo = quick overview
- Or record multiple narration versions

**Q: Can I edit the script?**
A: Absolutely! The script is a starting point. Customize it for your audience, industry, or presentation style.

**Q: What recording equipment do I need?**
A: See the "Recording Instructions" section in `AUDIO_RECORDING_SCRIPT.md`. A basic USB microphone ($50-100) is sufficient for good quality.
