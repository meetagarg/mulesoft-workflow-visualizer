**Problem 2: Robotic Voice**

## 💡 How to Use

### First Time Setup

1. **Refresh Browser** (Cmd+R or Ctrl+R)
2. **Check Voice Dropdown** - Select a premium voice (⭐)
3. **Click Test Button** - Verify audio works
4. **Ensure Audio ON** - Green button means audio enabled
5. **Click Play** - Enjoy the demo!

### Recommended Settings

**Best Quality:**
- **Voice:** Samantha ⭐ (Mac) or Google US English Female
- **Speed:** 1x
- **Browser:** Safari (Mac) or Chrome

**Quick Demo:**
- **Voice:** Any premium voice ⭐
- **Speed:** 1.5x or 2x
- **Browser:** Any modern browser
